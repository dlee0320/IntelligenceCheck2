Introduction

Dungeons and Dragons, a role-playing game developed by Wizards of the Coast, is a powerful, fantastic experience. Or at least it should be. Adventurers may be able to rescue innocent victims of a goblin raid, emerge victorious from terrible wars, and conquer the most formidable enemies, but cannot overcome the problem of a forgotten play materials.

IntelligenceCheck is our solution to this problem - a mobile app which essentially lifts the traditionally paper-and-pencil setting of D&D to an intuitive digital interface.

The App

IntelligenceCheck is a mobile application for iOS that serves as a centralized digital interface for D&D play. The app is comprised of three main interfaces:

1) The Character Sheet

2) The Dungeon Master Guide/Player's Handbook

3) the Interactive Map/Combat Interface.

The app will also have wi-fi capabilities to allow players to share and view information as members of customizable parties for convenience in play.
